Tools built for structural coordination.

Clash detection

Hard and soft clash detection (50mm clearance tolerance) between structural and MEP elements. Export clash reports as CSV for RFI workflows.

3D section box

Interactive Min/Max X/Y/Z sliders to isolate any part of the building model for coordination review. Fits automatically to model extents.

Layer-controlled access

Structural engineers get write access to structural layers only. Architectural and MEP layers are visible but protected from accidental edits.

Quantity takeoffs

Automated concrete, steel, and reinforcement quantities from structural elements. Export to Excel for cost estimation and BOQ.

IFC4 round-trip

Import the architect's IFC model, overlay your structural framing, and export combined IFC4 with IfcStructuralMember classifications and load cases.

Section cuts & annotations

Cut any section through the 3D model. Add structural annotations, load diagrams, and detail callouts directly in the same drawing environment.
