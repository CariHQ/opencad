Stay informed without becoming an expert.

3D building visualization

Explore your building in real-time 3D. Navigate room by room, adjust the lighting, and get a true sense of scale and spatial quality before construction starts.

Design approval workflow

Your architect shares a link. You review the design, add comments, and approve or request changes — all tracked with full version history.

Progress tracking

See construction progress mapped against the original design model. Compare planned vs. actual for milestones, area completion, and materials used.

Contextual comments

Leave comments pinned to specific rooms, elements, or drawing locations. Your design team sees them in context — no more email threads about "the second window on the left".

View-only access

You see everything but can't accidentally modify the design. Your role is protected — the tools you don't need simply don't appear.

Works everywhere

Access your project from any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. No app to install, no plugins, no Autodesk account required.
