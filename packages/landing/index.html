Capabilities

Everything a BIM
tool should be.

Built from the ground up for the browser. Runs offline, syncs in real time, and integrates with every format your team uses.

2D & 3D drafting

Full-featured drafting tools — walls, doors, windows, slabs, roofs — with snap, trim, and parametric constraints.

Real-time collaboration

CRDT-backed multiplayer editing. Every cursor, every change, live — with conflict-free merging and version history.

AI design assistant

Describe a floor plan in natural language. The AI generates geometry, assigns materials, and checks building codes automatically.
