Pricing FAQ

Do you really offer a free forever plan?

Yes. Starter is free for up to 3 projects and never expires. You can upgrade at any time without losing your work.

Do I need a credit card for the 14-day trial?

No. Paid plans include a 14-day trial that starts the moment you upgrade — no card required until you decide to continue.

Can I downgrade?

Yes. Downgrade or cancel any time from your account settings. Projects that exceed the Starter limit stay accessible but read-only until you upgrade again.

Is there an annual discount?

Annual billing is ~20% off the monthly rate. Pick the cadence at checkout — contact sales@opencad.archi for 50+ seats.

What about education / open-source licenses?

OpenCAD is open-source (Apache-2.0) and you're welcome to self-host the full source. Hosted Pro is free for verified students — email edu@opencad.archi with your .edu address.
